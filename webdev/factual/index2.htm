Fwix

  • By: Fwix
  • Platform: iOS, Web App
  • Submitted: 2011-06-09
  • License: Free
  • Features: POI Data
Visit App Site Visit Developer Site

Description

This app is an example of our technology that powers local data (places, news, events, reviews, photos, and more) for Partners and Developers. The app provides real-time local news on your iOS device. Our website also features our technology, showing you real-time local information for your city.

Developer Info

Fwix organizes the world’s information by location. With our geotagging technology we are able to automatically index the web by latitude and longitude coordinates. Our technology covers 61,000 cities in 7 countries, and we are constantly growing. We are the largest local content curator, collecting content from over 35,000 sources that includes news, blogs, events, reviews, social media, and more. We provide local data to Partners and Developers who use our API and Local Tools.
