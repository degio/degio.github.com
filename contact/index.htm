→ Design Philosophy

Good Design:

  • Is innovative.
  • Should make a product useful.
  • Is aesthetic design.
  • Will make a product understandable.
  • Is honest.
  • Is unobtrusive.
  • Is long-lived.
  • Is consistent in every detail.
  • Is environmentally friendly.
  • Is as little design as possible.

- Dieter Rams
